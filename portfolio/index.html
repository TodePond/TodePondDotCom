SandPond, 2019

I made SandPond, a 3D voxel sandbox. I also made SpaceTode, a spatial programming language for creating elements in its simulation by drawing before-and-after pictures. I documented my progress in a series of videos known as the SandPond Saga. SandPond is now complete, but the saga continues to this day!

  • How to make water in sandpond

CellPond, 2021

I created CellPond based on everything I learned from SandPond. Unlike SandPond, you can code it entirely by dragging-and-dropping shapes around in its visual programming interface.

Sandspiel Studio, 2022

I created Sandspiel Studio with Max Bittker. It's a simulation game that lets users create and modify elements by dragging-and-dropping blocks in its visual editor. There's a gallery for sharing, exploring and remixing creations.

tldraw's Enhanced Presence, 2022
